# Integration Research for SleekInvoices

## Whop Platform Overview

**Website:** https://whop.com
**Tagline:** "Where the internet does business"
**Total Payouts:** $2.3+ billion to creators

### Key Features:
- All-in-one platform for building and monetizing online businesses
- Low transaction fees (starting at 2.7% + $0.30)
- Categories: Trading, Sports, Social media, Clipping, Reselling, Real estate
- Supports digital products, subscriptions, memberships, and communities
- Affiliate/referral program capabilities

### Potential Integration Value:
- Could enable SleekInvoices users to sell invoice templates
- Affiliate program for user referrals
- Community features for freelancers/agencies
