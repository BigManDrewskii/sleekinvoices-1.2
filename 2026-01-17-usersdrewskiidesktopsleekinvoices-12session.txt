     items

     - Best for: Clean, minimal aesthetic

     - Inactive hover: Add background + slight border (bg-accent/20)

     - Click: Noticeable scale + color shift                                    
     - Best for: Balanced visibility and aesthetics                             
                                                                                
     Option 3: High Contrast                                                    
                                                                                
     - Inactive hover: Strong background (bg-accent/30)                         
     - Active hover: Significantly lighter + shadow                             
     - Click: Pronounced animation                                              
     - Best for: Maximum usability and feedback
